The most populous city in Nigeria and the

The Major Financial Centre.

The city of Lagos is a major economic focal point in Nigeria, generating around 10% of the country's GDP

Most commercial and financial business is carried out in the central business district situated on the island

This is also where most of the country's commercial banks, financial institutions and major corporations are headquartered

The Lagos Culture

Music

Lagos is famous throughout Africa for its music scene, Lagos has given birth to a variety of styles such as juju, fuji an Afrobeats

Movie

Lagos is the centre of the Nigerian movie industry, often referred to as 'Nollywood'. Idumota market on Lagos Island is the primary distribution centre

Arts

The National Arts Theatre, located at Iganmu is home to the primary centre for the performing arts and artistes in Nigeria
